### Syllogistic Paradox

Deals Imaginary DMG to a single target enemy and applies Wiseman's Folly. When Dr. Ratio's allies attack a target afflicted with Wiseman's Folly, Dr. Ratio launches 1 follow-up attack on this target.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 90*
