## Skills

### I Want to Help

Deals minor Physical DMG to a single enemy.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 30*

### Svarog Watches Over You

Deals Physical DMG to all enemies. Additionally deals Physical DMG to targets with Marks of Counter.

*Weakness Break : AoE : 30*

### Promise, Not Command

Reduces DMG received, increases chance to be attacked by enemies, and enhances Counters.

*Weakness Break : 0*

### Because We're Family

DMG received from enemy attacks is reduced. Enemies who attack Clara will be marked with a Mark of Counter and met with Svarog's Counter, dealing Physical DMG.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 30 / Blast : 30*

### A Small Price for Victory

Attacks the enemy. After entering battle, this character's chance of being attacked by enemies increases.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 60*
