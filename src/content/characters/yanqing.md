Increases Yanqing's CRIT Rate. Enhances "Soulsteel Sync" and deals massive Ice DMG to a single enemy.

### One With the Sword

During Soulsteel Sync, reduces the chance of this character being attacked and increases their CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG. *Weakness Break : Single Target : 30*

*Weakness Break : 0*
