## Overview
A senior manager in the IPC Strategic Investment Department and one of the Ten Stonehearts, known for her cornerstone "Jade of Credit."A cold and elegant moneylender, she is skilled at understanding the human heart, with a personal hobby called "Bonajade Exchange."She's willing to wait patiently for high-value acquisitions and adept at extracting value from seemingly destitute clients.

## Skills

### Lash of Riches

Deals Quantum DMG to a single enemy and minor Quantum DMG to enemies adjacent to it.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 30 / Blast : 15*

### Acquisition Surety

Makes a single ally become a Debt Collector and increases their SPD. After a Debt Collector attacks, they deal minor Additional Quantum DMG to each enemy target hit and consume their own HP. When Jade becomes a Debt Collector, she cannot gain the SPD boost effect, and her attacks do not consume HP.

*Weakness Break : 0*

### Vow of the Deep

Deals Quantum DMG to all enemies and gains increased DMG multiplier for follow-up attack.

*Weakness Break : AoE : 60*

### Fang of Flare Flaying

After Jade or a Debt Collector ally attacks, Jade gains 1 point of Charge for each enemy target hit. When the Charge reaches #3[i] points, consumes the #3[i] points to launch 1 instance of follow-up attack, dealing Quantum DMG to all enemies.When Jade launches follow-up attacks, gains Pawned Asset and increases CRIT DMG, stacking up to #2[i] times.

*Weakness Break : AoE : 30*

### Visionary Predation

Inflicts Blind Fealty on all enemies within a set area. Attacking an enemy with Blind Fealty causes all enemies with Blind Fealty to enter combat simultaneously. Upon entering combat, deals minor Quantum DMG to all enemies and immediately gains #3[i] stack(s) of Pawned Asset.

*Weakness Break : 0*
