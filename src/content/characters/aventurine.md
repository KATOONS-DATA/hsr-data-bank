## Skills

### Straight Bet

Deals minor Imaginary DMG to a single target enemy.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 30*

### Cornerstone Deluxe

Provides all allies with a Fortified Wager shield, whose Shield effect is stackable.

*Weakness Break : 0*

### Roulette Shark

Gains a random amount of Blind Bet points and inflicts Unnerved on a single enemy, dealing Imaginary DMG. When an ally hits an Unnerved enemy, the CRIT DMG dealt increases.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 90*

### Shot Loaded Right

For any single ally with Fortified Wager, their Effect RES increases, and when they get attacked, Aventurine accumulates Blind Bet. When Aventurine has Fortified Wager, he can resist Crowd Control debuffs. Upon reaching 7 points of Blind Bet, Aventurine consumes the 7 points to launch a follow-up attack that deals minor Imaginary DMG to random single enemy targets, bouncing a total of 7 times.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 10*

### The Red or the Black

Using the Technique randomly grants one out of the three DEF Boost effects with different buff values. After entering the next battle, increases all allies' DEF by the corresponding value.

*Weakness Break : 0*
