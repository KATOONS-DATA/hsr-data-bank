## Overview

## Skills

### Novaburst

Deals minor Quantum DMG to a single enemy.

*Weakness Break : Single Target : 30*

### Known by Stars, Shown by Hearts

Activates Matrix of Prescience. DMG received by Fu Xuan's allies are Distributed to her. Also increases CRIT Rate and Max HP of all allies.

*Weakness Break : 0*

### Woes of Many Morphed to One

Deals Quantum DMG to all enemies and increases Fu Xuan's Talent trigger count.

*Weakness Break : AoE : 60*

### Bleak Breeds Bliss

While Fu Xuan is still active in battle, the DMG taken by all team members is reduced.When her HP is low, automatically restores her own HP based on the percentage of HP already lost. This effect can have up to 2 trigger counts at any given time.

*Weakness Break : 0*

### Of Fortune Comes Fate

Activates a Barrier. Allies will not enter battle when attacked by enemies. Entering battle will automatically activate Matrix of Prescience.

*Weakness Break : 0*

## Eidolons

### E1 — Dominus Pacis

The Knowledge effect increases CRIT DMG by 30%.

### E2 — Optimus Felix

If any team member is struck by a killing blow while Matrix of Prescience is active, then all allies who were struck by a killing blow during this action will not be knocked down, and 70% of their Max HP is immediately restored. This effect can trigger 1 time per battle.

### E3 — Apex Nexus

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

### E4 — Fortuna Stellaris

When other allies under Matrix of Prescience are attacked, Fu Xuan regenerates 5 Energy.

### E5 — Arbiter Primus

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

### E6 — Omnia Vita

Once Matrix of Prescience is activated, it will keep a tally of the total HP lost by all team members in the current battle. Fu Xuan's Ultimate DMG will increase by 200% of this tally of HP loss.This tally is also capped at 120% of Fu Xuan's Max HP and the tally value will reset and re-accumulate after Fu Xuan's Ultimate is used.
